# Book Architecture

## Working Title

**The Deep Studio**  
*Designing Hypnotic Electronic Music*

## Estimated Finished Length

Target range: **250–350 pages**.

The exact page count will be determined by the quality and usefulness of the material rather than by an arbitrary target.

## Parts

| Part | Title | Approx. Pages | Purpose |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| I | The Listener | 35–45 | Attention, perception, hypnotic movement |
| II | Studio Architecture | 45–60 | Hardware roles, routing, signal flow |
| III | Ableton Live Workflow | 35–50 | Template, returns, resampling, printing |
| IV | Sound Design | 55–75 | Pads, bass, texture, granular, hardware processing |
| V | Composition & Arrangement | 30–45 | Long-form structure, energy, movement |
| VI | Mixing, Depth & Space | 35–50 | Frequency, stereo, distance, weight |
| VII | The Library | 20–35 | Personal sample library and sonic identity |
| Appendices | Reference | 25–40 | Routing charts, glossary, checklists |
