# Acknowledgements

This book grows from a fascination with deep, restrained and evolving electronic music: music that can be listened to for a long time without becoming static, music that rewards attention, music that feels physical, spacious and alive.

It is also shaped by the instruments, processors and workflows of a particular studio. The hardware and software discussed throughout the book are not treated as status objects, but as tools for making decisions.

Future acknowledgements will be expanded as the manuscript develops.

## Influences

The book is informed by traditions of dub techno, deep 140, ambient music, sound design, studio-as-instrument practice, resampling workflows and long-form electronic composition.

The aim is not imitation.

The aim is understanding.
