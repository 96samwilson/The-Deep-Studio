# Chapter 1 — Callout Examples

:::key-idea
The producer's true medium is not sound alone. It is attention through time.
:::

:::studio-mission
Create a ten-minute recording from a single sustained sound. Do not add another instrument. Create interest only through Weight, Distance, Movement and Focus.
:::

:::listening-exercise
Choose one reference track and write down every moment where your attention shifts. Describe the experience without naming equipment or effects.
:::

:::avoid
Do not solve boredom by adding material before exploring whether the existing sound can evolve.
:::
