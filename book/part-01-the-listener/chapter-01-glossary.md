# Chapter 1 — Glossary

## Attention

The listener's focus over time.

## Continuous Transformation

Gradual, ongoing change that prevents music from becoming static.

## Weight

Perceived mass, density, warmth and solidity.

## Distance

Perceived depth, ambience, spatial placement and separation.

## Movement

The sense that a sound is alive, changing or evolving.

## Focus

The element or dimension currently holding the listener's attention.

## Print

To record a sound or processing chain to audio so it becomes material rather than a live possibility.

## Studio Mission

A practical assignment designed to turn the chapter's ideas into studio behaviour.
