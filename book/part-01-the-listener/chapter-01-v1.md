# Chapter 1 — The Studio Begins With Listening

> Every studio contains two signal paths. One carries audio. The other carries attention.

## Purpose

This Version 1.0 manuscript assembles the concepts developed across Commits 0004–0027 into a single editorial baseline.

## The Central Thesis

The producer's medium is not sound alone; it is the listener's attention through time.

Technology exists to shape perception.

## Core Frameworks

- Deep Listening Framework
- Frequency as Perception
- Time as Perception
- Attention Mapping

## Studio Practice

Each technical decision should be evaluated by asking:

1. What is the listener experiencing?
2. Which listening dimension should change?
3. Can the existing material evolve instead of adding something new?

- Attention Map
